My friends introduce me to table tennis. I fell in love with it and started playing with them. I still don't have my personal racket at this time so I just borrow with them.

Certified Athlete

Changed directions

I watched Code.org's YouTube video about Computer Programming and was moved by the impact computer science give to our society and decided to do the same - code and try to have an impact.

Wanting to do more
